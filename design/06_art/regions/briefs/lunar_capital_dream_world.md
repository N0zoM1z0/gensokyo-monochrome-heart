# Lunar Capital and Dream World — Visual Production Brief

Source narrative bible: `03_locations/lunar_capital_dream_world.md`  
Production tier: **B**  
Profile family: **B → D**  
Region stamp: **crescent inside a sleeping eye**

## Visual thesis

Sterile certainty and unruly dreams become frightening when each impersonates the other.

## Recognition lock

- Silhouette vocabulary: clean lunar spires, rabbit antennae, fan and sword lines, soft dream bubbles and three-world orbs.
- Landmark: a perfect moon gate casts a soft, anatomically impossible sleeping shadow.
- Texture rule: capital uses sparse exact line grids; dream world uses slow large dither blobs, never pixel noise.
- Entry transition: an eyelid-shaped iris closes; on opening, grid and dream profiles swap.

## Four depth bands

1. **FAR:** white vacuum or black dream field.
2. **MID:** precise capital terraces / soft impossible horizons.
3. **PLAY:** geometric bridges / deforming dream floors.
4. **FRONT:** scan frames, bubbles and foreground moon gates.
