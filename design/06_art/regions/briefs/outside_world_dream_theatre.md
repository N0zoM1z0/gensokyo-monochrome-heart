## Visual thesis

A memory of the outside world is a performance with visible missing scenery.

## Recognition lock

- Silhouette vocabulary: rail platform, classroom window, city antennae, smartphone, occult cape and exposed theatre braces.
- Landmark: a city skyline is visibly held up by two backstage braces and one dream thread.
- Texture rule: screen-tone blocks imitate print/phone capture; stage remains clean enough to reveal artifice.
- Entry transition: a phone camera shutter freezes the set; the frozen image slides aside.

## Four depth bands

1. **FAR:** black auditorium void with white city pinpoints.
2. **MID:** flat scenery cards: train, school, rooftop.
3. **PLAY:** stage floor, taped marks and dream platforms.
4. **FRONT:** curtain edge, phone screen, dangling cable and audience shadow.

- ROUTE: one prop gains a Gensokyo repair and survives scene changes
