Production tier: **B**

Region stamp: **open rectangle behind a folding fan**

## Visual thesis

Every backstage entrance reveals an author, but never the entire production.

## Recognition lock

- Silhouette vocabulary: freestanding doors, fan arcs, seated deity triangle, season emblems and impossible frame overlaps.
- Landmark: four doors show four seasons while casting one shared black shadow.
- Texture rule: profile shifts per doorway; threshold areas stay pure white for state readability.
- Entry transition: the current background closes like a door, revealing the next scene already behind it.

## Four depth bands

1. **FAR:** blank field with off-grid door specks.
2. **MID:** repeating stages seen through mismatched apertures.
3. **PLAY:** temporary platforms and threshold strips.
4. **FRONT:** door slabs crossing every UI-safe margin except the text zone.

## Ambient motion

hinge tick, season particle swap, dancer shadow and fan-snap inversion. All loops are 4/6/8/12 frames at 8 fps and must be individually disableable.

## World-state set

- CALM: doors obey a visible seasonal grammar
- INCIDENT: destinations and shadows disagree
- ROUTE: one door gains a private hand-painted mark
- SEASON: all four are present; active season controls only particles
- AFTER: backstage braces remain visible and proudly functional

## Combat and interaction readability

door teleports show destination outline for 12 frames; profile inversions never occur during dense volleys; screen border is gameplay geometry only when double-lined. Dialogue-safe zone is x=8–311, y=108–171; keep active faces and landmark pivots outside it during conversations.
