## Visual thesis

Military geometry tries to contain souls that refuse to become identical.

## Recognition lock

- Silhouette vocabulary: stacked black towers, assembly lines, regimented haniwa ranks, enormous beast-shadow cutouts.
- Landmark: Keiki atelier: a white goddess-shaped gap surrounded by dense black scaffolding.
- Texture rule: orthogonal 50% ordered dither interrupted by organic soul curves.
- Entry transition: a haniwa stamp strikes; the screen breaks into four disciplined panels.

## Four depth bands

1. **FAR:** white void with sparse descending soul commas.
2. **MID:** factory terraces and distant regiment silhouettes.
3. **PLAY:** hard-edged platforms, clay ramps and conveyor seams.
4. **FRONT:** passing haniwa shields and beast claws.
