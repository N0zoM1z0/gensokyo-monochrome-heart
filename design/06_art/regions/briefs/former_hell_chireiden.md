# Former Hell and the Palace of the Earth Spirits — Visual Production Brief

Source narrative bible: `03_locations/former_hell_chireiden.md`  
Production tier: **A**  
Profile family: **D → C**  
Region stamp: **third eye above a reactor ring**

## Visual thesis

What is buried remains warm, social and capable of answering back.

## Recognition lock

- Silhouette vocabulary: cavern vaults, palace arches, cat cart, raven wing, reactor circles and third-eye cords.
- Landmark: subterranean sun: concentric white rings held inside a nearly solid black reactor hall.
- Texture rule: 75% rock masses, 25% steam, broad white safety lanes around interactables.
- Entry transition: a third-eye cord draws a circle; the circle becomes a tunnel or reactor iris.

## Four depth bands

1. **FAR:** black cavern with white heat cracks.
2. **MID:** old city bridges and palace facade.
3. **PLAY:** basalt ledges, tiled corridors and reactor catwalks.
4. **FRONT:** steam plumes, chains, pipes and passing spirits.

## Ambient motion

heat shimmer as alternating contour, cat-tail flame, cable pulse and slow spirit ascent. All loops are 4/6/8/12 frames at 8 fps and must be individually disableable.

## World-state set

- CALM: warm windows dot the black city
- INCIDENT: heat cracks connect into an accusing eye
- ROUTE: one sealed palace room opens into a quiet domestic vignette
- SEASON: surface weather appears only as pipe condensation
- AFTER: safety markings remain hand-painted and slightly uneven

## Combat and interaction readability

reactor pulses occur behind the playfield at 2 Hz maximum; bullets use white cores with black safety rims; camera shake is optional and disabled by default. Dialogue-safe zone is x=8–311, y=108–171; keep active faces and landmark pivots outside it during conversations.
