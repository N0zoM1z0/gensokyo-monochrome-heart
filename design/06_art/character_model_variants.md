## Additional modeling experiments

### Paper-doll profile
Dialogue-only busts built from replaceable eye, mouth, hand, and prop layers. Cheap to author, excellent for localization timing, but must avoid mechanical puppet stiffness.

### Shadow-theatre profile
Solid silhouettes with one white eye line for dream scenes. Strong mood and low asset count. Not suitable for all ordinary dialogue.

### Woodblock profile
Thicker contour, carved hatching, limited frame animation. Ideal for chapter cards, rumor illustrations, and historical retellings.

### LCD ghost profile
One-pixel horizontal gaps, delayed afterimage, and segment-like facial marks. Reserved for Outside World phone memories and Archive corruption.

### Super-deformed festival profile
12 × 16 mascots for Journal stamps, minigame crowds, and route-completion decorations. Never substitute these for serious afterbeats.

## Production recommendation

Use Model M for 90% of exploration. Model S is a derived manual redraw, not an automatic shrink. Model L is built only for eight launch fighters at first. Portraits share accessory design sheets but are not enlarged sprites.
