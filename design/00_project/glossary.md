# Project Glossary

- **Spot:** a selectable point inside a region that can host one or more events.
- **Spot Event:** the atomic authored unit combining story and play.
- **Afterbeat:** a quiet, short scene after mechanical escalation.
- **Memory Thread:** a route-linked sequence of events.
- **Resonance:** relationship progression based on mutual understanding, not a visible love meter.
- **Margin:** resource earned by grazing danmaku; represents space made inside a crowded situation.
- **Incident Condition:** a temporary fighter rule tied to location or story.
- **Fanon Dial:** permitted intensity of a community joke in a character's portrayal.
- **Agent Skill File:** a `skills.md` document specifying how a character-writing agent should reason, speak, remember, and refuse mischaracterization.
- **Deep Route:** a six-to-eight-event romance-capable arc.
- **Support Route:** a shorter friendship-focused arc.
- **Ensemble Accord:** optional postgame route requiring respectful resolution across multiple deep routes.
- **One-bit:** two visual values only: black and white. Apparent gray is produced by controlled dithering.
