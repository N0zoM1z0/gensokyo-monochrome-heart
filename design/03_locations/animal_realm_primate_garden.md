# Animal Realm and Primate Spirit Garden
## 畜生界・霊長園

## Canon anchor

The Animal Realm is dominated by competing beast-spirit organizations; the Primate Spirit Garden is protected by Keiki and her haniwa creations.

This file uses the anchor as a foundation and treats all spot layouts and incidents below as project-original.

## Narrative thesis

**Treating people as resources creates efficient systems and empty loyalties.**

## Exploration grammar

Faction permissions, command pressure, high-speed routes, crafted helpers, and city geometry.

### Companion affordances

At least three companions in this region must alter traversal in different ways. A companion action should reveal personality or relationship state; it must not function as a generic colored key.

### State readability

The region needs four visible world-state changes:
1. an incident-state alteration;
2. a route-private alteration;
3. a seasonal alteration;
4. a post-finale “imperfect but alive” alteration.

## Fighting stage

Animal city intersection; Faction Pressure condition rewards holding territory.

## Event hooks

- Yachie negotiates a memory as if it were labor.
- Saki challenges a route map to a race.
- Keiki creates an idol that refuses its assigned purpose.
- Mayumi recognizes the protagonist as an unauthorized command source.

## 1-bit art direction

angular city blocks, beast emblems, oil surfaces, clay figures, fortress garden walls

### Dither allocation

- 0%: important white space, sky, or quiet emotional focus
- 25%: distant atmosphere
- 50%: traversable but uncertain space
- 75%: danger, rumor, or occluded depth
- 100%: solid silhouette and collision-critical geometry

Never use high-frequency dither directly behind bullets or dialogue text.

## Audio direction

Hard driving rhythm for city; clay percussion and stately creator motif for garden.
