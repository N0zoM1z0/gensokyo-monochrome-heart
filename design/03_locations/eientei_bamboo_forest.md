# Eientei and the Bamboo Forest of the Lost
## 永遠亭・迷いの竹林

## Canon anchor

Eientei lies deep in the Bamboo Forest of the Lost and is associated with Kaguya, Eirin, Reisen, Tewi, and earth rabbits.

This file uses the anchor as a foundation and treats all spot layouts and incidents below as project-original.

## Narrative thesis

**Eternity becomes meaningful only when moments can differ and end.**

## Exploration grammar

Topology changes with sound, wavelength, moon phase, and memory. Visual landmarks are intentionally unreliable but never unfair.

### Companion affordances

At least three companions in this region must alter traversal in different ways. A companion action should reveal personality or relationship state; it must not function as a generic colored key.

### State readability

The region needs four visible world-state changes:
1. an incident-state alteration;
2. a route-private alteration;
3. a seasonal alteration;
4. a post-finale “imperfect but alive” alteration.

## Danmaku language

False moons, wavelength bands, medicine droplets, rabbit ricochets, fire phoenix trails, and repeating phases with one altered detail.

## Fighting stage

Long moonlit corridor and bamboo clearing; False Moon condition can mirror marked inputs.

## Event hooks

- Four versions of dawn occupy one corridor.
- A patient remembers a treatment not yet given.
- Kaguya requests an impossible object that already sits beside her.
- Mokou burns a path that reappears behind the party.

## 1-bit art direction

vertical bamboo rhythm, long black corridors, white moon disks, medicine shelves, rabbit ears in negative space

### Dither allocation

- 0%: important white space, sky, or quiet emotional focus
- 25%: distant atmosphere
- 50%: traversable but uncertain space
- 75%: danger, rumor, or occluded depth
- 100%: solid silhouette and collision-critical geometry

Never use high-frequency dither directly behind bullets or dialogue text.

## Audio direction

Looping ostinato with one changing note; clinic cue uses dry clicks, night cue stretches time.
