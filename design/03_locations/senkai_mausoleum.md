# Senkai and the Hall of Dreams' Great Mausoleum
## 仙界・夢殿大祀廟

## Canon anchor

A hermit realm associated with Toyosatomimi no Miko and her Taoist companions, adjoining the mausoleum awakened in Ten Desires.

This file uses the anchor as a foundation and treats all spot layouts and incidents below as project-original.

## Narrative thesis

**Hearing desire is not permission to define it.**

## Exploration grammar

Overlapping desire signals, feng-shui paths, temporary wall passages, and archaic mechanisms.

### Companion affordances

At least three companions in this region must alter traversal in different ways. A companion action should reveal personality or relationship state; it must not function as a generic colored key.

### State readability

The region needs four visible world-state changes:
1. an incident-state alteration;
2. a route-private alteration;
3. a seasonal alteration;
4. a post-finale “imperfect but alive” alteration.

## Danmaku language

Desire spirits, plate arcs, wall-entry vectors, rigid hopping grids, and royal light rays.

## Fighting stage

Audience chamber with Desire Amplification condition; repeated moves reveal intention and become easier to counter.

## Event hooks

- Miko hears a desire generated by the Archive rather than a person.
- Futo arranges a perfect room nobody can enter.
- Seiga offers a useful shortcut with an unspoken cost.
- Yoshika remembers a poem that belongs to the future.

## 1-bit art direction

stone tomb passages, halo lines, plates, star maps, impossible doors

### Dither allocation

- 0%: important white space, sky, or quiet emotional focus
- 25%: distant atmosphere
- 50%: traversable but uncertain space
- 75%: danger, rumor, or occluded depth
- 100%: solid silhouette and collision-critical geometry

Never use high-frequency dither directly behind bullets or dialogue text.

## Audio direction

Bright ceremonial lead, asymmetric plate percussion, hollow mausoleum resonance.
