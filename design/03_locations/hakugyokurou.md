# Hakugyokurou
## 白玉楼

## Canon anchor

A vast estate in the Netherworld, home to Yuyuko and Youmu, with gardens, spirits, and the great cherry tree Saigyou Ayakashi.

This file uses the anchor as a foundation and treats all spot layouts and incidents below as project-original.

## Exploration grammar

Two-layer traversal coordinates physical space and phantom currents. Petal timing reveals hidden paths.

### Companion affordances

At least three companions in this region must alter traversal in different ways. A companion action should reveal personality or relationship state; it must not function as a generic colored key.

### State readability

The region needs four visible world-state changes:
1. an incident-state alteration;
2. a route-private alteration;
3. a seasonal alteration;
4. a post-finale “imperfect but alive” alteration.

## Danmaku language

Petal curtains, delayed sword cuts, ghost lanes, appetite orbs, and patterns that only clear when the player crosses rather than retreats.

## Fighting stage

Training yard with Spring Snow condition; floatier jumps and delayed phantom strikes.

## Event hooks

- A single petal refuses to land.
- A meal repeats before its last bite.
- A spirit has memorized the wrong farewell.
- Youmu has maintained one garden path out of existence.

## 1-bit art direction

white stone steps, black cherry branches, drifting spirit tails, raked garden lines, kitchen steam

### Dither allocation

- 0%: important white space, sky, or quiet emotional focus
- 25%: distant atmosphere
- 50%: traversable but uncertain space
- 75%: danger, rumor, or occluded depth
- 100%: solid silhouette and collision-critical geometry

Never use high-frequency dither directly behind bullets or dialogue text.

## Audio direction

Airy 1-bit waltz, soft plucked rhythm, sudden silence before sword accents.
