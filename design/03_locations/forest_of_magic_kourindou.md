# Forest of Magic and Kourindou
## 魔法の森・香霖堂

## Canon anchor

A hazardous magical forest associated with Marisa and Alice; Kourindou is Rinnosuke's curio shop near its edge.

This file uses the anchor as a foundation and treats all spot layouts and incidents below as project-original.

## Narrative thesis

**Curiosity becomes care when objects and people are allowed to have histories beyond usefulness.**

## Exploration grammar

Spores, light shafts, doll pathways, and outside-world objects create optional routes. Inventory clutter is environmental storytelling.

### Companion affordances

At least three companions in this region must alter traversal in different ways. A companion action should reveal personality or relationship state; it must not function as a generic colored key.

### State readability

The region needs four visible world-state changes:
1. an incident-state alteration;
2. a route-private alteration;
3. a seasonal alteration;
4. a post-finale “imperfect but alive” alteration.

## Danmaku language

Stars, lasers, doll formations, thread lines, spores, and misidentified object behavior.

## Fighting stage

Forest clearing with Magic Miasma condition; projectiles leave temporary spore zones.

## Event hooks

- A returned book refuses to leave Marisa's shelf.
- A doll performs a gesture Alice never taught it.
- Rinnosuke identifies a device's purpose incorrectly but its name correctly.
- An Outside World object contains a monochrome photo of the shrine.

## 1-bit art direction

gnarled trunks, mushrooms, shelves, bottles, doll silhouettes, heaps of unrecognized tools

### Dither allocation

- 0%: important white space, sky, or quiet emotional focus
- 25%: distant atmosphere
- 50%: traversable but uncertain space
- 75%: danger, rumor, or occluded depth
- 100%: solid silhouette and collision-critical geometry

Never use high-frequency dither directly behind bullets or dialogue text.

## Audio direction

Curious walking bass in chiptune form; bright star burst for action; clockwork doll counterline.
