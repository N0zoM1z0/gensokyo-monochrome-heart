# Heaven
## 天界

## Canon anchor

A celestial realm associated with celestials, peaches, keystones, and Tenshi Hinanawi; access is exceptional.

This file uses the anchor as a foundation and treats all spot layouts and incidents below as project-original.

## Narrative thesis

**A flawless life can become a prison when nothing resists or surprises.**

## Primary cast

- Tenshi
- Iku
- Shion visitors
- celestials

## Spots

- Cloud Stair
- Peach Orchard
- Keystone Terrace
- Celestial Banquet
- Weather Observatory
- Edge of Heaven

## Exploration grammar

Cloud buoyancy, keystone gravity, weather currents, and intentionally over-perfect geometry.

### Companion affordances

At least three companions in this region must alter traversal in different ways. A companion action should reveal personality or relationship state; it must not function as a generic colored key.

### State readability

The region needs four visible world-state changes:
1. an incident-state alteration;
2. a route-private alteration;
3. a seasonal alteration;
4. a post-finale “imperfect but alive” alteration.

## Signature minigames

- Keystone Construction
- Peach Banquet Etiquette
- Atmosphere Reading
- Cloud Surf

## Danmaku language

Earth shocks above clouds, scarlet temperament lines, lightning messengers, and falling keystone shadows.

## Fighting stage

Keystone terrace with Temperament Storm condition.

## Event hooks

- A perfect view repeats until it becomes invisible.
- Tenshi builds an obstacle solely to have someone object.
- Iku reads a social atmosphere that does not match anyone present.
- One peach ages while everything else remains unchanged.

## 1-bit art direction

large white cloud masses, black keystones, sparse palace rails, peach silhouettes, enormous empty sky

### Dither allocation

- 0%: important white space, sky, or quiet emotional focus
- 25%: distant atmosphere
- 50%: traversable but uncertain space
- 75%: danger, rumor, or occluded depth
- 100%: solid silhouette and collision-critical geometry

Never use high-frequency dither directly behind bullets or dialogue text.

## Audio direction

Open high register, low earth-impact percussion, playful imperious fanfare.
