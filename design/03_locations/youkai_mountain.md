# Youkai Mountain
## 妖怪の山

## Canon anchor

A major mountain region associated with tengu, kappa, waterfalls, forests, and the Moriya Shrine near its upper areas.

This file uses the anchor as a foundation and treats all spot layouts and incidents below as project-original.

## Narrative thesis

**Information and infrastructure can connect people or turn them into resources.**

## Exploration grammar

Wind vectors, elevation, patrol lines, restricted paths, water pressure, and newspaper rumors change routes.

### Companion affordances

At least three companions in this region must alter traversal in different ways. A companion action should reveal personality or relationship state; it must not function as a generic colored key.

### State readability

The region needs four visible world-state changes:
1. an incident-state alteration;
2. a route-private alteration;
3. a seasonal alteration;
4. a post-finale “imperfect but alive” alteration.

## Danmaku language

Curved wind lanes, photo frames, leaf fans, water jets, patrol grids, and rotating misfortune spirals.

## Fighting stage

Waterfall ledges and gust-assisted aerial movement; Mountain Gale condition.

## Event hooks

- A photograph depicts tomorrow's accident.
- A patrol order names no intruder.
- A rumor duplicator becomes more convincing each time it is denied.
- Hina gathers a curse that seems to remember the protagonist.

## 1-bit art direction

steep rock silhouettes, white waterfall columns, rope bridges, maple canopy, pipes and waterwheels, distant crow shapes

### Dither allocation

- 0%: important white space, sky, or quiet emotional focus
- 25%: distant atmosphere
- 50%: traversable but uncertain space
- 75%: danger, rumor, or occluded depth
- 100%: solid silhouette and collision-critical geometry

Never use high-frequency dither directly behind bullets or dialogue text.

## Audio direction

Fast wind percussion and camera-click accents; alternate calm ridge cue for private conversations.
