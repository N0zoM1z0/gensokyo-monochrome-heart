# Lunar Capital and Dream World
## 月の都・夢の世界

## Canon anchor

The Lunar Capital is a highly ordered lunar civilization; Dream World is governed by dream logic and associated with Doremy Sweet.

This file uses the anchor as a foundation and treats all spot layouts and incidents below as project-original.

## Narrative thesis

**Purity and perfect order can erase the value of mixed, mortal experience.**

## Exploration grammar

Dream scene transitions, purity filters, spoken-word reversals, sea/mountain links, and multiple world layers.

### Companion affordances

At least three companions in this region must alter traversal in different ways. A companion action should reveal personality or relationship state; it must not function as a generic colored key.

### State readability

The region needs four visible world-state changes:
1. an incident-state alteration;
2. a route-private alteration;
3. a seasonal alteration;
4. a post-finale “imperfect but alive” alteration.

## Danmaku language

lunar grids, divine summons, dream sheep curves, purified pattern cores, reversed outcome phrases, three-world swaps.

## Fighting stage

Moon sea balcony or dream arena; Pure Ground condition removes stage clutter but amplifies mistakes.

## Event hooks

- A dream remembers the protagonist more accurately than waking characters.
- Sagume refuses to describe the incident because speech would reverse it.
- The Watatsuki sisters classify affection as contamination.
- Doremy discovers a dream with no dreamer.

## 1-bit art direction

clean geometric corridors, moon disks, sea-wave symbols, dream clouds, sparse court ornament

### Dither allocation

- 0%: important white space, sky, or quiet emotional focus
- 25%: distant atmosphere
- 50%: traversable but uncertain space
- 75%: danger, rumor, or occluded depth
- 100%: solid silhouette and collision-critical geometry

Never use high-frequency dither directly behind bullets or dialogue text.

## Audio direction

Cold precise pulse contrasted with warm unstable dream harmony.
