# Scarlet Devil Mansion
## 紅魔館

## Canon anchor

A western-style mansion near Misty Lake, home to Remilia, Flandre, Sakuya, Patchouli, Meiling, fairy maids, and Koakuma.

This file uses the anchor as a foundation and treats all spot layouts and incidents below as project-original.

## Exploration grammar

Doors, clocks, servant routes, and etiquette permissions reshape traversal. Some rooms exist in stopped-time layouts.

### Companion affordances

At least three companions in this region must alter traversal in different ways. A companion action should reveal personality or relationship state; it must not function as a generic colored key.

### State readability

The region needs four visible world-state changes:
1. an incident-state alteration;
2. a route-private alteration;
3. a seasonal alteration;
4. a post-finale “imperfect but alive” alteration.

Clock hands, knife lattices, scarlet fog silhouettes, elemental book glyphs, and crystalline break-points.

## Fighting stage

Grand hall with balcony drops and Red Mist condition; breakable furniture resets between spell-card rounds.

## Event hooks

- A corridor loses one minute every dusk.
- A book records tomorrow's conversation.
- Flandre can see a breakable point in an idea.
- Remilia's predicted guest arrives with the wrong memory.

## 1-bit art direction

gothic arches, tall windows, checker floors rendered with low-frequency dither, clocks, books, curtains, service doors

### Dither allocation

- 0%: important white space, sky, or quiet emotional focus
- 25%: distant atmosphere
- 50%: traversable but uncertain space
- 75%: danger, rumor, or occluded depth
- 100%: solid silhouette and collision-critical geometry

Never use high-frequency dither directly behind bullets or dialogue text.

## Audio direction

Chamber pulse, harpsichord-like chiptune, restrained clock percussion; reference the relevant mansion themes only in licensed arrangements.
