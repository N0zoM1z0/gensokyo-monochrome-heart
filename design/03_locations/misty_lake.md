# Misty Lake
## 霧の湖

## Canon anchor

A mist-covered lake near the Scarlet Devil Mansion, associated with fairies, Cirno, and the mansion's approach.

This file uses the anchor as a foundation and treats all spot layouts and incidents below as project-original.

## Narrative thesis

**Simple confidence can be creative rather than merely foolish.**

## Primary cast

- Cirno
- Daiyousei cameo
- Wakasagihime cameo
- Meiling patrols
- Scarlet residents

## Spots

- Fog Shore
- Frozen Reeds
- Fairy Island
- Boat Wreck
- Mansion Causeway
- Moon Reflection

## Exploration grammar

Visibility, ice formation, sound reflection, and moving fog banks create short puzzle spaces.

### Companion affordances

At least three companions in this region must alter traversal in different ways. A companion action should reveal personality or relationship state; it must not function as a generic colored key.

### State readability

The region needs four visible world-state changes:
1. an incident-state alteration;
2. a route-private alteration;
3. a seasonal alteration;
4. a post-finale “imperfect but alive” alteration.

## Signature minigames

- Perfect Freeze Puzzle
- Fog Echo Tag
- Fairy Territory Flags

## Danmaku language

Ice crystals, fog-hidden bullets with safe outlines, ricochet lanes, and playful chase patterns.

## Fighting stage

Frozen shore with cracking ice; Perfect Freeze condition creates temporary platforms.

## Event hooks

- The lake freezes around an unfrozen memory.
- Cirno is the only person who notices a pattern is too perfect.
- A boat returns with no passenger but a warm teacup.
- The mansion's reflection shows a missing room.

## 1-bit art direction

broad white fog, black reeds, broken ice polygons, distant mansion silhouette

### Dither allocation

- 0%: important white space, sky, or quiet emotional focus
- 25%: distant atmosphere
- 50%: traversable but uncertain space
- 75%: danger, rumor, or occluded depth
- 100%: solid silhouette and collision-critical geometry

Never use high-frequency dither directly behind bullets or dialogue text.

## Audio direction

Playful square-wave motif with icy noise percussion; quiet fog drone at night.
