# Outside World Dream Theatre
## 外の世界・夢劇場

## Canon anchor

A postgame dream-stage representation of modern Japan and the supplied Outside World episodes. It is explicitly a memory theatre, not a factual map of current Japan.

This file uses the anchor as a foundation and treats all spot layouts and incidents below as project-original.

## Narrative thesis

**Convenience and spectacle cannot replace shared presence.**

## Primary cast

- broad ensemble

## Spots

- Small Apartment
- Convenience Store
- Station Concourse
- Akihabara Hall
- Event Convention
- Office Floor
- Night Rooftop

## Exploration grammar

Crowd navigation, device misunderstanding, limited apartment space, and culture-clash interactions.

### Companion affordances

At least three companions in this region must alter traversal in different ways. A companion action should reveal personality or relationship state; it must not function as a generic colored key.

### State readability

The region needs four visible world-state changes:
1. an incident-state alteration;
2. a route-private alteration;
3. a seasonal alteration;
4. a post-finale “imperfect but alive” alteration.

## Signature minigames

- Convenience Store Shift
- Smartphone Rumor Chain
- Convention Booth Planner
- Office Meeting Survival
- Apartment Tetris

## Danmaku language

UI notification bullets, train timetable lanes, convention flyer storms; always framed as dream comedy.

## Fighting stage

Rooftop dream stage with Signal Loss condition.

## Event hooks

- A device recognizes a character by a fan nickname.
- A convention sells a memory no one consented to publish.
- The apartment contains three keepsakes after everyone leaves.
- A search engine returns a blank page for the protagonist.

## 1-bit art direction

train signs, compact room furniture, screens, vending machines, convention tables; no real trademarks in final art

### Dither allocation

- 0%: important white space, sky, or quiet emotional focus
- 25%: distant atmosphere
- 50%: traversable but uncertain space
- 75%: danger, rumor, or occluded depth
- 100%: solid silhouette and collision-critical geometry

Never use high-frequency dither directly behind bullets or dialogue text.

## Audio direction

City pulse made from chiptune notification sounds; ending strips back to room ambience.
