# Former Hell and the Palace of the Earth Spirits
## 旧地獄・地霊殿

## Canon anchor

The underground Former Hell includes the Palace of the Earth Spirits, home to Satori and her pets, and the Former Hell of Blazing Fires.

This file uses the anchor as a foundation and treats all spot layouts and incidents below as project-original.

## Exploration grammar

Thought echoes appear as textless icons. Some objects change only when not directly observed. Heat routes and rails structure action.

### Companion affordances

At least three companions in this region must alter traversal in different ways. A companion action should reveal personality or relationship state; it must not function as a generic colored key.

### State readability

The region needs four visible world-state changes:
1. an incident-state alteration;
2. a route-private alteration;
3. a seasonal alteration;
4. a post-finale “imperfect but alive” alteration.

## Event hooks

- Satori hears a memory the protagonist does not own.
- Koishi leaves evidence of a conversation nobody recalls.
- Orin's cart carries a living rumor.
- Utsuho mistakes the Archive for a new sun.

## 1-bit art direction

brick caverns, palace eye motifs, rail tracks, furnace circles, old-city lanterns

### Dither allocation

- 0%: important white space, sky, or quiet emotional focus
- 25%: distant atmosphere
- 50%: traversable but uncertain space
- 75%: danger, rumor, or occluded depth
- 100%: solid silhouette and collision-critical geometry

Never use high-frequency dither directly behind bullets or dialogue text.

## Audio direction

Low industrial pulse, heartbeat motifs, playful cat rhythm, overwhelming reactor chord made readable in one-bit sound.
