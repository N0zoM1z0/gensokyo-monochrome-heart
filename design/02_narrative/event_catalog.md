## Optional seasonal event seeds

- Tanabata: wishes are accidentally printed as facts.
- Summer festival: every stall uses a different local mechanic.
- Moon viewing: false moon causes route memories to swap owners.
- Halloween: costumes temporarily alter companion AI.
- Sports day: Incident Conditions become athletic rules.
- Art festival: portraits reveal what the painter omits.
- Christmas: gift labels are replaced by rumor labels.
- New Year: offerings replay the previous year's choices.
- Reitaisai dream: characters encounter fan interpretations of themselves.
- Outside World technology: a search engine indexes Gensokyo memories.

## Dream Theatre events

School, idol, gender-role reversal, wedding photography, and corporate AU episodes are presented as explicitly non-canon “Dream Theatre” records. They can be broad, self-aware, and fanon-heavy without contaminating main-continuity characterization.
