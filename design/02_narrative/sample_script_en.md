# Sample Script — English
## EVT-HKR-001 “The Empty Cushion”

**Context:** Prologue morning. The protagonist has just returned. Reimu claims the second cushion is incidental.

---

`[Hakurei Shrine — Veranda — Morning]`

**REIMU:** You're awake.

**PROTAGONIST:** I think so.

**REIMU:** That's not a very useful answer.

**MARISA:** It is if this is still a dream.

`[Marisa leans through the open screen. A leaf is caught in her hat.]`

**REIMU:** Use the entrance.

**MARISA:** I did. The entrance was too slow.

**PROTAGONIST:** You both remember me.

`[One beat. Reimu lifts her teacup.]`

**REIMU:** I remember a lot of troublesome things.

**MARISA:** I remember you promising to help me catalogue mushrooms.

**PROTAGONIST:** I promised no such thing.

**MARISA:** See? Exactly the same.

`[The player notices a second cushion. It has been repaired with new thread.]`

### Tone choice

- **Direct:** “You fixed that for me.”
- **Playful:** “Does every mysterious visitor get reserved seating?”
- **Patient:** Sit without commenting.
- **Defiant:** “You could simply say you're glad I came back.”

### Direct response

**REIMU:** I fixed a cushion. Don't make it important.

**PROTAGONIST:** It already is.

**REIMU:** Then you're the one making trouble.

`[A black stain crawls beneath the donation box.]`

**MARISA:** Speaking of trouble—

**REIMU:** I saw it.

**MARISA:** I was going to say that.

**REIMU:** You were going to shout it.

`[CONTROL RETURNS]`

Objective: inspect the donation box, the repaired cushion, and the boundary stain.

---

## Afterbeat

`[Later. The veranda is quiet. Marisa has left to “borrow” a broom.]`

**REIMU:** Your tea's cold.

**PROTAGONIST:** I can make another.

**REIMU:** Don't. This one's fine.

`[She slides the repaired cushion two pixels closer.]`

**REIMU:** The path gets dark early.

**PROTAGONIST:** Is that a warning?

**REIMU:** It's information.

**PROTAGONIST:** And the spare bedding?

**REIMU:** Also information.

`[Journal unlocked: “An empty place can be a kind of invitation.”]`
