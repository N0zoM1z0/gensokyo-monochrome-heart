# Canon Source Register

This register is a research map, not a distribution bundle. The project contains no copied game data, scans, music, or scripts.

## Official game/profile sources by production cluster

### Core / early Windows cast
- *Embodiment of Scarlet Devil* — Reimu, Marisa, Cirno, Meiling, Koakuma, Patchouli, Sakuya, Remilia, Flandre.
- *Perfect Cherry Blossom* — Letty and Prismriver context where needed; Chen, Alice, Youmu, Yuyuko, Ran, Yukari.
- *Imperishable Night* — Keine, Tewi, Reisen, Eirin, Kaguya, Mokou; major Reimu/Marisa/Alice/Remilia/Yuyuko team dialogue.
- *Phantasmagoria of Flower View* — Aya, Medicine, Komachi, Eiki, Yuuka returns, broad cross-cast dialogue.

## Official print / book sources

- *Curiosities of Lotus Asia* — Rinnosuke, Marisa, Reimu, Outside World objects, ordinary Gensokyo texture.
- *Bohemian Archive in Japanese Red* — Aya's reporting style and interviews; treat reporter framing as biased in-world evidence.
- *Perfect Memento in Strict Sense* — Akyuu's encyclopedia; useful but explicitly an in-world human chronicle.
- *Symposium of Post-mysticism* — faction interviews and profiles; also in-world rhetoric, not neutral omniscience.
- *Forbidden Scrollery* — Kosuzu, Human Village, dangerous books, Reimu/Marisa everyday dynamics.
- *Wild and Horned Hermit* — Kasen, Reimu, shrine life, social/economic incidents.
- *Lotus Eaters* — alcohol/food establishment context and later everyday characterization.
- *The Grimoire of Marisa* / *The Grimoire of Usami* — spell-card observation and event framing.
- *Cage in Lunatic Runagate* / *Silent Sinner in Blue* — Lunar Capital, Eirin, Kaguya, Reisen, Watatsuki sisters, Yukari.
- official manga and magazine works released after this register's snapshot should be checked when relevant.

## In-world narrator caution

Official print books often have in-world authors with bias:
- Akyuu writes for human safety and social order.
- Aya writes as a reporter seeking a story.
- Marisa writes as a magician evaluating spell cards.
- symposium speakers promote factions.

The project treats these as valuable perspectives, not absolute psychological truth.

## Source use in this package

Character profiles summarize anchors and provide source categories without reproducing official text. Original sample dialogue is clearly marked original. Final writers should keep private research notes with exact work/chapter/route references for high-impact scenes.
