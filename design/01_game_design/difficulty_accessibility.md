# Difficulty and Accessibility

## Global presets

### Story
For players prioritizing narrative:
- slower bullets and larger telegraphs;
- generous fighter guard;
- minigame timers extended;
- navigation hints on;
- optional auto-Focus;
- no content penalty.

### Standard
Intended first-play balance.

### Veteran
Denser patterns and stricter mechanical scoring, without more story rewards.

### Custom
Independent sliders.

## Visual

- high-contrast outline thickness
- reduced dither motion
- no checkerboard flashes
- flash intensity
- screen shake
- hit-stop
- CRT curvature and scanlines off by default
- UI scale 100–200%
- dyslexia-friendly spacing option for English
- Japanese line-height and ruby-text toggle where used
- bullet shape legend independent of color

## Motor

- full remapping
- hold/toggle Focus
- hold/toggle rapid fire
- one-handed dialogue mode
- simplified fighter inputs
- minigame slow mode
- repeated-action reduction
- analog dead-zone settings

## Cognitive

- event recap
- relationship recap in plain language
- map objective pin
- puzzle hints in three levels
- dialogue history
- pause during most minigames
- no surprise permanent lockouts

## Audio

- independent music, SFX, ambience, voice-chirp controls
- visual rhythm cues
- subtitle-safe sound notifications
- mono audio option

## Content

- romance per character toggle
- flirt intensity
- reduced alcohol references
- mortality chapter warning
- ensemble-route toggle
